PTZ Camera Controls

Mouse Controls:
Left-click and drag to pan and tilt the camera.
Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out.

Direct Value Entry:
Click on any value (Pan, Tilt, Zoom) to type in an exact value.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • W Tilt up
  • A Pan left
  • S Tilt down
  • D Pan right
  • + Zoom in
  • - Zoom out
  • R Reset camera position
